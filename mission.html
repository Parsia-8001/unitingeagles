The mission of Uniting Eagles is the pursuit of the following principles:

  • Commitment: Uniting Eagles wants to inspire pro-social friendships, strong interpersonal skills, and instill a sense of hope in the future.

  • Responsibility: The focus of Uniting Eagles is to empower youth in establishing goals and following through on commitments.

  • Possibility: Uniting Eagles wants to expand the perspective of young people to make them aware of life's possibilities.

  • Support: An individual is dramatically influenced by their support system. Uniting Eagles wants to surround young people in a caring, inclusive learning environment.
